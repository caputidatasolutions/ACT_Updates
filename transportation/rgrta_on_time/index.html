RGRTA (RTS) On-Time Performance

transportationUpdated Jun 17, 2026

This indicator shows the percentage of Rochester-Genesee Regional Transportation Authority (RGRTA) buses that are on-time. RGRTA includes Regional Transit Service (RTS) bus services. On-time performance includes the percentage of buses that leave their start point or arrive at a destination within 2:59 minutes early to 5:59 minutes late.
